The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in the 1870s England, when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and vigorously maintained English estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
